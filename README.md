# -SUPPLY-CHAIN-PERFORMANCE-AND-SHIPMENT-ANALYSIS-EFFICIENCY
 This project analyzes supply chain operations using Power BI to identify shipment inefficiencies, demand trends, and operational performance gaps across different regions and product categories.

The objective of the analysis was to evaluate demand patterns, shipment delays, and logistics performance in order to provide actionable business insights that can improve supply chain responsiveness and operational efficiency.

## Business Problem
 Supply chain inefficiencies such as shipment delays, uneven regional demand, and inconsistent delivery performance can negatively impact customer satisfaction and operational costs.
This analysis focuses on identifying:

•	High-demand regions and products

•	Shipment modes with poor delivery performance

•	Regional logistics inefficiencies

•	Overall shipment reliability

## Dashboard Features
•	Demand analysis by region and product category

•	Monthly order quantity trends

•	Shipment delay analysis by shipment mode and customer region

•	On-time vs delayed shipment performance

•	Executive KPI metrics for operational monitoring

•	Interactive slicers for regional and product-level filtering

## Key Insights
•	Central America and Western Europe recorded the highest customer demand.

•	Clothing and outdoor product categories generated the largest order quantities.

•	Shipment delays accounted for a significant portion of deliveries, indicating operational inefficiencies.

•	First Class shipping experienced the highest average shipment delays despite being a premium delivery option.

•	Order quantities declined significantly toward the end of the year, suggesting possible seasonal demand fluctuations.

## Recommendatuion 
•	Improve shipment scheduling and route optimization to reduce delivery delays.

•	Review logistics performance for First Class shipments.

•	Prioritize operational planning for high-demand regions and product categories.

•	Use historical demand trends to improve forecasting and inventory planning.

•	Continuously monitor shipment KPIs using interactive dashboards.

## Overrall Conclusion
The supply chain analysis revealed significant insights into customer demand patterns, shipment efficiency, and operational performance across different regions and product categories. The dashboard showed that customer demand is highly concentrated in Central America and Western Europe, while clothing and outdoor-related products generated the highest order volumes.

Shipment performance analysis identified operational inefficiencies across shipment modes and customer regions. A large proportion of shipments experienced delays, with First Class shipments recording the highest average shipment delays despite being a premium shipping option. Regional variations in shipment performance also highlighted inconsistencies in logistics efficiency and delivery responsiveness.

The analysis further revealed a decline in order quantities toward the later months of the year, suggesting possible seasonal demand fluctuations and the need for improved forecasting and operational planning.

Overall, the findings demonstrate the importance of continuously monitoring shipment performance, demand trends, and logistics operations in order to improve supply chain efficiency, reduce delivery delays, optimize resource allocation, and enhance customer satisfaction.
